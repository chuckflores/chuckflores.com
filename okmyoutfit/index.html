    OkMyOutfit

    On-Boarding & Home Page Optimization

    OkMyOutfit is an on-demand personal styling service offering unlimited stylist appointments for a monthly fee.

    Problem

    Their current on-boarding process was very long, taking users on average 10 mins to complete. In addition to a confusing on-boarding questionaire, their homepage didn't clearly describe the service to visitors.

    Changing The Service

    After user interviews and tests it was discovered that users wanted the stylist to take their measurements at the first visit. This lead to giving the user the option of requesting in-home measurements and style assessment or completing the sign up form and trying on cloths the first visit.

    POSITIVE & ENGAGING QUESTIONS

    We redesigned the style profile questions to be clear in their language and promote positive body image. Additionally we supplemented the questions with a secondary copy. This clarified the question and told the user why the information is needed.
